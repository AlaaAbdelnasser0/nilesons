Admissions
Steps

  • 1. Complete an Application form
  • 2. You will be notified with the ‘Assessment & Parents’ Interview Date’ through a phone call/email
  • 3. You need to bring the registration documents needed “see below” on the interview day
  • 4. Once the student is accepted, you will kindly pay the fees


Registration
Documents

  • 1. Original birth certificate
  • 2. Six personal pictures 4x6
  • 3. A copy of parent’s national ID & a photo for each parent
  • 4. The last school certificate he/she received “stamped”
  • 5. An approval letter from the embassy to study in our school for an Egyptian national returnee or an expatriate in Egypt
