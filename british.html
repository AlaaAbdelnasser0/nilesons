British Division

  • Academics
  • British Division

Our British Division's curriculum is aligned to the Common Core State Standards.

Primary stage curriculum is building on the skills and learning experience our students were exposed to in the foundation stage.

Literacy, Numeracy, Science/Health, Art, ICT, Music and Physical Education plus French or German, Arabic and Religion. These subjects are introduced to students through differentiated learning activities to ensure the maximum benefit for each and every student. In lower secondary, the curriculum is designed to build on what has been studied in the Primary Stage. At the end of Year 8, students will sit for another check Point Exam. At this point, students will be ready for the IGCSE program. In Year 9, students will study the Core curriculum and will sit for the Core exam which is a preparation for Year 10 to study the Extended Curriculum. To complete the IGCSE, students are required to be examined in 8 or more subjects along with AS/A levels and the choice based on the university’s requirements. In addition to the IGCSE subjects, students are required to study Arabic, Religion and Civil Studies to meet the Ministry of Education requirements.

Of course we also follow the requirements of the Egyptian Ministry of Education for Arabic, Religion and Arabic Social Studies according to the national curriculum.
